    Speed of light in a vacuum:
      \begin{equation}
        c  =  3.0 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s} \tag{in a vacuum}
    \end{equation}
    Speed of light where $n$ is the index of refraction:
    \begin{equation}
      v = \frac{c}{n} \tag{speed of light}
    \end{equation}
    Law of refraction:
\begin{equation}
  n_1 \sin \theta_1 = n_2 \sin \theta_2 \tag{Snell's Law}
\end{equation}
